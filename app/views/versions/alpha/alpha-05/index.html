Details of changes and things being tested in this iteration/version of the prototype

To understand current behaviour around balance information and understanding of the concept of changing the deduction amount in the context of an on-benefit repayment journey & whether people would use such service.

  • What the "your debt" means in the context of the prototype?
  • Do they understand that the deductions come out of their benefits?
  • Why would they change the amount?
  • What else do people need to know while changing the payments?
  • Explanation page – what sort of information do people think they need to provide here?
  • Amended end date after changing the deduction amount – does it play a part in people’s decision making towards the amount the fill in
  • Minimum payment amount – do people go for minimum amount?
  • What would they expect to happen next?
  • Would they expect to be notified & how?
  • How quickly they expect it to happen?
  • What information they expect that notification to have?
  • How do people respond to debt breakdown info?
  • Does it help them to understand why they have a debt?
  • If not what would help if yes why is it helpful?
  • What would they do next? WHY?
