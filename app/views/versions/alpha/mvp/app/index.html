Manage your benefit repayments

You can use this service to:

  • get details of benefits you have to pay back
  • understand how you're paying the money back
  • ask for repayments to be changed to suit your financial circumstances (you'll need to give details of your income, savings and any outstanding debt)

You can only use this service if you've been given a username and password from Department for Work and Pensions.
